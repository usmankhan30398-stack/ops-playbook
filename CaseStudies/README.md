# Case Studies: Real-World AI Applications

This folder contains documented case studies showcasing how AI can drive strategy and operational improvements. Each case study focuses on real-world scenarios and actionable insights:

### Case Study 1: Customer Support Automation
- **Challenge**: Reducing response times for customer queries.
- **Solution**: 
  - Integrated OpenAI’s GPT model for automated FAQs and query resolution.
  - Scenario: Chatbot support for a retail e-commerce platform.
- **Impact**: Reduced average response time to under 10 seconds; increased customer satisfaction score (CSAT) by 25%.

### Case Study 2: Supply Chain Optimization
- **Challenge**: Predicting demand to manage inventory shortages and excess.
- **Solution**: 
  - Used AI forecasting with time-series models (e.g., Azure ML) and visualization tools like Tableau.
  - Integrated Slack notifications for real-time supplier updates.
- **Impact**: Achieved a 20% increase in on-time delivery rates by optimizing inventory flow.

### Case Study 3: Strategic Decision-Making via Predictive Analytics
- **Challenge**: Understanding customer churn patterns and reducing attrition.
- **Solution**: Leveraged predictive analytics in Google Sheets to highlight accounts at risk and deployed AI to recommend proactive actions.
- **Impact**: Improved customer retention rates by 15%.
