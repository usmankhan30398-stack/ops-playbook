# # Case Studies: Real-World AI Applications

This folder contains documented case studies highlighting how AI can drive strategy and operational improvements. Each case study focuses on practical scenarios and demonstrates actionable insights.

---

### Case Study 1: Customer Support Automation
- **Challenge**: A retail e-commerce platform experienced long response times and inconsistent customer support across queries related to order statuses, refunds, and product information.  
- **Solution**: Integrated OpenAI’s GPT-based chatbot, which automated responses for FAQs, tracked order statuses using API calls, and escalated complex issues to human agents as needed.  
- **Implementation Process**:
  1. Connected the chatbot to the company’s CRM system.
  2. Trained the bot using historical customer inquiries to ensure it could answer accurately.
  3. Set up fallback handling to escalate queries beyond the bot’s scope.  
- **Impact**: Achieved a 40% reduction in response time, resolved 80% of inquiries without agent involvement, and improved customer satisfaction (CSAT) by 25%.

---

### Case Study 2: Supply Chain Optimization
- **Challenge**: A manufacturing company faced difficulties in predicting demand accurately, leading to frequent overstock and stockouts. This resulted in unnecessary storage costs and delays in fulfilling orders.  
- **Solution**: Deployed a time-series forecasting AI model using tools like Azure Machine Learning. The model analyzed historical sales data, seasonality, and market trends to provide accurate demand predictions on a monthly basis.  
- **Implementation Process**:
  1. Collected and preprocessed data from ERP systems and past sales logs.
  2. Trained the AI model to predict demand, factoring in variables like seasonality and external influences (e.g., holidays).
  3. Created Slack alerts to notify the supply chain team of potential shortages or surpluses, enabling just-in-time restocking.  
- **Impact**: Increased inventory turnover by 15% and reduced storage costs by 20% while consistently meeting delivery deadlines.

---

### Case Study 3: Predictive Analytics for Strategic Decision-Making
- **Challenge**: A SaaS company struggled with customer retention, as they lacked insights into why customers were churning and how to take proactive steps to address their needs.  
- **Solution**: Leveraged AI-powered predictive analytics to forecast churn risk. Using historical customer usage data, billing trends, and support tickets, the AI model identified customers most likely to churn within 90 days.  
- **Implementation Process**:
  1. Built a machine learning model using customer lifetime usage data, NPS scores, and billing histories as input features.
  2. Generated churn risk scores for each customer and flagged high-risk accounts for the sales team.
  3. Suggested personalized interventions (e.g., discounts, feature walkthroughs) to mitigate churn risks.  
- **Impact**: Boosted customer retention by 12% over three quarters and increased upsell opportunities among “high churn risk” accounts.
